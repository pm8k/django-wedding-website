The Proposal

On December 30th, 2016, Matt and Megan arrived in Zurich, Switzerland after coming from skiing in St. Anton the previous days, and before that Vienna, Austria for Matt’s brother’s wedding. They decided to walk around Zurich to take in the sights and the culture. Matt pointed out that they should eat at a nice fancy spot named Terrasse, which Megan ALMOST vetoed despite Matt making a reservation for it months in advance. They ended up going. Though a really cool beer hall was a close second!


The dinner at Terrasse was phenomenal where everything seemed just perfect. Megan even commented on just that, as they walked towards a bridge overlooking Lake Zurich. Megan was pointing out how beautiful the Christmas tree was, and identifying all of the different birds on the lake - swans, ducks, grebes. After much stalling, much kissing, and Megan picking up all of the hints knowing this was probably it, Matt told her that he had one more Christmas present for her.


He pulls out a his grandmother’s diamond on a gold chain necklace and gets down on one knee and pops the question (throwing a Rocky Horror Picture Show quote in for good measure). Megan said YES! And the rest is history.
