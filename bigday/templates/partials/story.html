Our Story

In the beginning of 2014, Matthew and Megan met at a board game meetup at the Cinderblock Brewery. They bonded over board games, beer, and generally nerdy topics and soon found themselves spending hours in Megan's car chatting about everything they could think of. On a whim, Megan asked Matt if he'd like to join her for a rare and special Final Fantasy concert in Omaha, NE since he seemed as into that kind of thing as her. He said yes! The relationship blossomed from there - a great big flower of geekery and awesomeness.
